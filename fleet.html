Passenger Fleet

Mission-Matched Aircraft for Group and Executive Travel

We currently position two passenger aircraft options: the ERJ145 for high-capacity movements and the Hawker 400XP for faster executive trips.

Regional Airliner

ERJ145

The ERJ145 is configured as a 60-seat passenger platform in your operation profile, making it suitable for larger group movements where capacity and schedule control matter more than private-cabin luxury.

  • Passenger Configuration60 seats
  • Best ForGroup transport and shuttle-style missions
  • StrengthLower per-seat cost at higher loads
  • Usage ProfileCorporate groups, sports teams, event travel

Operational Advantages

  • Efficient for moving medium-to-large passenger groups on one aircraft.
  • Airliner-style cabin and baggage profile compared with small business jets.
  • Strong fit for repeat corridors and planned group logistics.
Light Executive Jet

Hawker Jet 400XP

The Hawker 400XP is configured for 7 executive seats and is optimized for private, time-critical missions where speed, privacy, and fast ground turnaround are the priority.

  • Passenger Configuration7 executive seats
  • Best ForSenior leadership and VIP travel
  • StrengthFast dispatch for smaller passenger loads
  • Usage ProfileExecutive day returns and urgent meetings

Operational Advantages

  • Higher privacy and premium cabin experience for leadership-level passengers.
  • Very strong for short-notice movements with minimal boarding complexity.
  • More flexible for smaller party missions than airliner-class aircraft.
Usage Comparison

Which Aircraft Fits the Mission

Choose ERJ145 When

  • You need to move larger groups on one rotation.
  • You want better seat economics for high passenger counts.
  • Your mission is schedule-reliable group transport.

Choose Hawker 400XP When

  • You need a dedicated executive platform for small groups.
  • You prioritize privacy, speed, and direct business mobility.
  • Your mission is high-priority point-to-point travel.
